Description It spends a lot of time scavenging for scraps of algae. Season Winter Location Rivers and Mt. Lake Sell Price No Star 120 Silver 150 Gold 180 The Lingcod is a fish that can be found in the river (in Pelican Town or Cindersap Forest) or the Mountain Lake during Winter. It may also randomly appear in Krobus' shop on Wednesdays for 200g or at the Traveling Cart for 360-1,000g. Tailoring[ ] Lingcod can be used in the spool of the Sewing Machine to create the Fishing Vest. It can be used in dyeing, serving as an orange dye at the dye pots, located in Emily's and Haley's house, 2 Willow Lane.
